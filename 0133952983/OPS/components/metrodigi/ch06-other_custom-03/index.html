Tap “Start” for a demonstration of a maze such as the one used in Tolman's experiments in latent learning. A rat is placed in the start box. The trial is over when the rat gets to the end box.

The first group is placed in the maze. They wander aimlessly until they randomly find the exit where they are given food as a reinforcer.
Tap Next to continue.

They are then placed in the maze over and over until they can run it without making any mistakes, each time getting the reinforcement.
Tap Next to continue.

Group 2 is similarly placed in the maze over and over but is never given a reinforcer.
Tap Next to continue.

Group 1 consistently runs the maze correctly.
Tap Next to continue.

Group 2 seems to always wander about and find the exit by accident.
Tap Next to continue.

After ten days group 2 is given the reinforcer upon completing the maze.
Tap Next to continue.

Almost immediately they can run the maze succesfully from memory.
Tap Reset to start over.
