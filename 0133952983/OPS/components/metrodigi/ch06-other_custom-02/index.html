There are four schedules of reinforcement, each with its own typical pattern of responding. The responses are cumulative, which means new responses are added to those that come before, and all graphs begin after the learned pattern is well established. Slash marks mean that a reinforcement has been given.
Graph shows lines plotting cumulative responses on the Y axis and time on the X axis.
