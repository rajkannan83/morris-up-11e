Quiz Questions

  1. Your roommate is attempting to deal with a particularly stressful set of events in her life. You wonder whether she is coping well or whether there is cause for concern. Which of the following criteria would be LEAST useful in making that judgment?

    1. Whether she is doing what society says people should do in those kinds of situations.

    2. Whether her behavior is realistically meeting the demands of the situation.

    3. Whether her behavior is interfering with the well-being of others around her.

    4. Whether her actions are effectively meeting her needs.

  2. Mary lives in a way that enhances her own growth and fulfillment regardless of what others think. In many ways, she is unconventional, is a creative thinker, and has close relationships with only a few chosen individuals. According to Maslow, Mary is most likely:

    1. a Type A personality.

    2. engaging in positive reappraisal.

    3. a well-adjusted, self-actualizing person.

    4. making excessive use of intellectualization.
